My Journey

A New Chapter Begins

M

As a single mum with a chronic health condition, and after years in nursing and experiencing severe burnout, I wanted something more flexible, meaningful and hopeful. I wanted a way out of the environment I was in.

During caring for my terminally ill mum, and after her passing, travel gave me a way to find myself again — and combine my love of helping people with my passion for holidays, research and creating memorable experiences.

This business has become part of a new chapter for me which I'm so excited about. My only regret is not starting this sooner.

Maria x

My Background

Nine Years In Nursing

I spent nine years working across cardiac, stroke, dementia, community, care home and palliative care settings before stepping back from clinical work to look after my own wellbeing. That experience taught me the real cost of burnout — and how important it is to build a life with more flexibility and choice.

Today I run my travel business alongside my partner Stuart, and I love helping clients plan trips that create real, lasting memories — while also supporting others who want to explore travel as a flexible income of their own.

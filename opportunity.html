How Does This Work?

Commission, Built Into Every Booking

Every time a holiday is booked, commission is built into the price. As an Independent Travel Agent, that commission comes back to you. Book for yourself, friends, family and customers — a flexible way to earn around your life.

So You Can Earn

Three Ways To Benefit

On your travel

Make more memories by using it to stretch your holiday budget, earning the commission back on your own bookings and enjoying industry insider benefits.

From your travel

Choose to earn a flexible, uncapped income that fits around what you already do, by booking travel for others and earning the commission — a perfect side-hustle or career.

By sharing

Choose to recommend the opportunity to a friend and create a passive, residual income helping others do the same as an independent travel agent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Do I need travel experience?

No travel experience is needed. You will receive full training and step-by-step support from day one.

Can I do this alongside my current job?

Absolutely. Many agents start part-time alongside existing jobs, building gradually at their own pace.

Can I use this for my own holidays?

Yes. As an agent you can book your own holidays and may benefit from exclusive travel perks and rates.

Is there any training?

Yes. Full training, online tools and supplier resources are all available to help you grow with confidence.

Do I have to work set hours?

No, it's completely flexible. You choose your own hours and work entirely around your lifestyle and commitments.

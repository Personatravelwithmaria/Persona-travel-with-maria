Success Stories

Join a community of like-minded travel lovers who support, share ideas and celebrate every milestone together.

Meet Petra

As a fashion freelancer and busy mum, life is full, so finding something that fits into small pockets of time was a gift. I jumped in and haven't looked back. Earning on my own holidays and getting access to competitive rates felt like travel's best kept secret, but it was the flexible, uncapped income that really caught my attention. Now I have effort based income, control over my hours and the freedom to work from anywhere. And honestly? The community is one of the best parts.

Meet Nida

I'm a full time mum and former teacher. For a while I was unsure what I wanted to do next — nothing truly felt right, until I discovered this business. I had no prior experience as a travel agent, but the training is incredible: self paced, on demand, and it's given me the freedom I'd been searching for. I can now travel more with my family while earning a meaningful income, and I still have the time and flexibility to care for them the way I always have.

Meet Bels

I'm a divorced mum of three teenage daughters, building a flexible income to secure our future on my terms. Since joining in January 2024, I've booked over £100,000 in travel and have loved growing my own successful team. Travel had always been my reset button — the thing that brought me back to what really matters. It's given me more than just income; it's given me freedom, direction and real confidence in our future.

Meet Caroline

After my separation, I still wanted to travel, make memories, and enjoy amazing holidays, and my oldest friend told me about an incredible opportunity she had stumbled across! Joining this team has really changed my life. I've made genuine friends, found an amazing community, and I'm now building a passive income alongside my main job. It's flexible, it fits around my job, kids, dogs and everything in between, and my only regret is not getting started sooner.

Meet Victoria

As a military wife, constantly starting over meant friendships were hard to hold onto. This business changed that — it's helped me reconnect with myself, rebuild my confidence, and feel part of something meaningful again. What surprised me most was everything beyond the travel: I found a community of people who feel like old friends, funny, kind, supportive, and always encouraging you to grow.

Meet Andrea & Darren

We are a company secretary and a Quantity Surveyor, who already run a successful construction company in central London. We joined this exciting independent travel agency business to turn our passion for luxury travel into a lifestyle. This business has given us freedom, flexibility, and the joy of working together doing what we love most — travelling the world and inspiring others to do the same.

Meet Gem

I joined because I needed a way out. My NHS career as a nurse was taking a real toll on my mental health, and it was starting to affect our home life and my ability to be fully present as a mum. My partner Joel supported me in stepping away and starting something completely new. What I didn't expect was the community — being surrounded by like-minded people, in a space that feels genuinely supportive, has been a huge part of this journey. For us, this has been more than just a new direction; it's been a real shift in how we see our future.
